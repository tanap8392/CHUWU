The Philosophy

Five Elements, One Story

Wu Xing (五行) describes the five forces — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water — that circulate through every birth chart. Most people carry more of some and less of others. CHUWU exists to gently restore what's missing, one note at a time.
